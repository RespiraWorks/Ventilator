You will need all the tools listed.

  • Using items from above BOM as well as:

    • 5mm heat shrink tubing [ew-shrink5]

  • Instructions:

    1. Insert the 4 pre-crimped cables [2] into the 2x2 Mega-Fit housing [6].

    2. Clip the crimped ends off the remaining free end of the cables.

    3. Slide 15mm of heat shrink onto each of the 2 cables that will go to the DIN receptacle.

    4. Slide on a few extra lengths of heat shrink tubing for cable management before you proceed.

    5. Solder the cables to the DIN receptacle [3] per the cable drawing. Apply the heat shrink to the junction.

    6. Crimp the QC terminals [4] per the drawing.

    7. Crimp the ring terminal [5] per the drawing.

    8. Do a gentle pull test to confirm proper seating of all the contacts.

    9. Shrink the remaining pieces of heat shrink to make the cable more manageable.
